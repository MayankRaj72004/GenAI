Beneath the blazing summer sky,
A leather ball begins to fly.
The willow sings with every stroke,
As dreams arise in clouds of smoke.
Eleven hearts stand brave and tall,
United by the crimson ball.
The crowd erupts with endless cheer,
Filling every soul with cheer.
A bowler runs with fiery pace,
Determined by a fearless chase.
The batter waits with focused eyes,
Ready to send the ball to skies.
A perfect drive through covers glides,
Like flowing rivers, smooth it rides.
A soaring six beyond the stand,
Brings roaring joy across the land.
A clever yorker strikes the base,
Leaving batsmen stunned in place.
The spinner weaves a magic spell,
Where wickets tumble, stories tell.
Slip fielders wait with patient grace,
Hoping for one perfect embrace.
A diving catch steals every breath,
Snatching triumph from certain death.
Every over shapes the fight,
Turning darkness into light.
Sweat and courage pave the way,
Through every challenging display.
Victories bloom from fearless will,
When every player climbs the hill.
Defeat may sting for just a while,
Yet hope returns with every smile.
Records fade, but spirit stays,
Inspiring countless future days.
Legends rise through toil and pain,
Earning glory, not by gain.
Children mimic every shot,
Dreaming of the perfect spot.
Streets become a grand arena,
Every child a true achiever.
The scoreboard tells a fleeting tale,
But passion never seems to fail.
Respect for rivals lights the game,
Honor matters more than fame.
Cricket teaches heart and soul,
Teamwork is the greatest goal.
From first bright dawn till setting sun,
Every match is never done.
Cricket lives in every heart,
A timeless game, a work of art.
